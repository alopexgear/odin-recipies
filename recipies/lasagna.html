description

Lasagna is a great dish for any occasion

  • ground beef
  • lasagna noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella cheese
  1. preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. cook noodles according to package instructions
  3. brown ground beef in a skillet
  4. add tomato sauce to the beef and simmer for 10 minutes
  5. layer noodles, beef sauce, and cheese in a baking dish
  6. repeat layers until all ingredients are used
  7. top with cheese and bake for 25-30 minutes
  8. let cool for 10 minutes before serving
