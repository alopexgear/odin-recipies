Pasta is a great dish for any occasion

  • spaghetti noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • garlic
  • basil
  1. boil water in a large pot
  2. add spaghetti noodles and cook according to package instructions
  3. drain the noodles and set aside
  4. in a large skillet, sauté garlic in olive oil until fragrant
  5. add tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes
  6. add cooked noodles to the sauce and toss to combine
  7. top with fresh basil and serve
